FAQ

All you need to know

1. We already have strong freight forwarders; can Jet Freight still help me?

Yes, not only do we offer the services of our quality partners, but we can help manage your existing transportation network. At times, we may offer to blend in alternative options if beneficial.

2. What should I do if my shipment is delayed or lost?

We are sorry to hear that and will do our utmost to help you find a solution. For personalized handling of the problem, we suggest that you to send a message via the contact form. Our teams will get back to you as soon as possible and will do everything in their power to return your shipment to you.

3. Can you handle customs formalities?

Yes. Jet Freight has dedicated team to handle customs formalities and arranges customs brokerage service for all levels of intricacy. The service includes Customs document preparation and delivery, management of the clearance process, import document preparation, obtaining regulatory documents etc. If you are looking for a logistics company to handle custom clearances, we are here to assist you.

4. I'd like to apply for a position with Jet Freight. Is there currently a vacancy?

Please visit our careers page to see if there are any available positions for you. Also, do follow our LinkedIn page to stay up to date on new job openings.
